About

SUMMARY

I am a self taught game programmer with 4 years of experience. I have published 2 games on the google play store, Fail Air, and A Bounce Game. The first 3 years of my game development journey was focused on the Unity3D game engine, and various software to assist in that. For the past year and many to come, I have been interested in graphics programming, and developed my current project, Lil Engie game engine. Throughout the years, I have also enjoyed the artistic side of games, such as modeling and texturing.
